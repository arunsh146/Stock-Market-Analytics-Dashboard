# Stock Market Analytics Dashboard

## Project Highlights
- Analyzed 497,000+ stock trading records
- Created 4 KPI cards
- Developed 5 interactive visualizations
- Implemented date and stock-level filtering
- Generated actionable market insights

## Objective
To analyze stock market performance using Power BI and create an interactive dashboard with KPIs, filters, and visualizations.

## Dataset
Stock Prices Data Set containing:
- Symbol
- Date
- Open Price
- High Price
- Low Price
- Close Price
- Volume

## KPIs
1. Total Trading Records
2. Highest Stock Price
3. Lowest Stock Price
4. Average Closing Price

## Visualizations
- Closing Price Trend
- Trading Volume Analysis
- High vs Low Price Comparison
- Top 10 Stocks by Closing Price
- Top Stocks by Trading Volume (Treemap)
- Date Filter
- Stock Symbol Filter

## Key Insights
- Total records analyzed: 497,472
- Highest stock price observed: 2.07K
- Lowest stock price observed: 1.50
- Average closing price: 86.37
- Stock prices generally showed an upward trend over time.
- Trading volume remained stable with occasional spikes.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI
- CSV Dataset

## Conclusion
The dashboard provides an interactive way to analyze stock market trends, trading volume, and stock performance. Users can filter data by date and stock symbol to gain deeper insights.
